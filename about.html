Mission

Clean.fit helps people achieve their fitness goals and maintain mind and body wellness while enjoying delicious, all-natural, and convenient snacks and supplements. It's our mission to promote a clean.fit life: the intersection of eating well, staying fit, and practicing self-love.

What’s in a clean.fit box?

Each month of your clean.fit box subscription, you'll receive 5-7 premium, hand-curated items: an assortment of non-GMO, clean and delicious fitness snacks plus all-natural, workout enhancing supplements and/or protein powders. Some items are full-size and some are trial size, but the retail value of the box will always exceed your monthly subscription cost!

Many of the items in the box are high in protein, organic, and grass-fed; but all of the items are produced using only high-quality, simple ingredients that support healthy living.

We also offer gluten-free and vegetarian clean.fit box options

Clean-eating and Paleo enthusiasts love our box, as it’s perfectly convenient for the on-the-go lifestyle!

What’s in a vegetarian clean.fit box?

All of the items in the vegetarian clean.fit box meet the same quality standards as our original and gluten-free boxes, of course; but they also meet the requirements of a lacto-ovo vegetarian diet.

This means the items in the vegetarian clean.fit box could contain egg and dairy ingredients, including vegetarian-friendly whey protein (meaning the enzymes used to cleave the protein are microbial or plant-based; not from animal sources). If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to email info@cleanfitbox.com.

What’s the history of clean.fit box?

clean.fit was started out of a passion for healthy living: eating well, staying fit, and practicing self-love. The founder, Renee Fouquet, was frustrated with the difficulty of finding snack items, protein, and workout supplements that aligned with all three tenets: so many products on the market today focus only on short-term “gains”. That wasn’t good enough - she only wanted to consume products that promote both sustainable long-term fitness and whole body wellness (all while tasting great, of course!).

clean.fit was born to help like-minded people discover new snack and workout supplement products - which are usually sourced from smaller, independent brands not found in big box stores. All of the items in the box are hand-curated and must meet our high quality standard - never including yucky ingredients!

When will I receive my clean.fit box?
